# **LUXE E-Commerce Design System: Modern Retail**

## **💎 Design Philosophy**

The LUXE aesthetic is defined by cleanliness, precision, and high-end minimalism. We prioritize white space and typography to elevate product imagery and create a sophisticated shopping experience.

## **🎨 Color Palette**

* Background: Pure White (\#FFFFFF) - The foundation for all screens.  
* Primary Action: Vibrant Orange (\#FF5722) - Used for "Add to Cart", "Sign In", and primary navigation states.  
* Accents: Soft Gold (\#D4AF37) - Used for highlights, ratings, and special status indicators.  
* Typography: Deep Onyx (\#1A1A1A) for headings; Cool Gray (\#6B7280) for body text.  
* Dividers/Borders: Light Smoke (\#E5E7EB) - 1px width.

## **✍️ Typography**

* Primary Font: Inter or Poppins.  
* Headings: Bold weight with generous letter spacing.  
* Body: Regular weight with optimized line height (1.5x) for readability.

## **📐 UI Components & Layout**

* Style: Flat design. Absolutely no glassmorphism or AI-style gradients. Clean white space is paramount.
* Rounding: 8-12px corner radius for buttons and cards.  
* Shadows: Strictly no heavy shadows. Use 1px borders for depth if necessary.  
* Grid: 16px standard gutter/padding for mobile layouts.

## **🚫 Style Constraints**

* NO Gradients.  
* NO Glassmorphism.  
* NO Soft/Heavy shadows.  
* NO Clutter. Focus on high-res product assets.
